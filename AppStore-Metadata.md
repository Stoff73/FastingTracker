# Fasting Tracker - App Store Metadata

## App Store Description

**Transform your health with mindful intermittent fasting**

Fasting Tracker is your personal companion for intermittent fasting, designed to help you build sustainable fasting habits while connecting with friends on the same journey.

### ✨ Key Features

**Smart Tracking**
- Real-time fasting timer with beautiful visual progress
- Multiple fasting stage milestones (12h, 16h, 18h, 24h+)
- Mood tracking throughout your fasting journey
- Detailed progress analytics and statistics

**Social Connection**
- Add friends and share your fasting progress
- Motivate each other with real-time status updates
- Privacy-first approach - you control what you share

**Intelligent Notifications**
- Smart milestone alerts based on fasting stages
- Motivational reminders to keep you on track
- Customizable notification preferences
- Never miss important fasting moments

**Educational Content**
- Learn about the science of intermittent fasting
- Understand different fasting stages and their benefits
- Tips and insights from fasting experts
- Evidence-based health information

**Privacy & Security**
- All data stored securely on your device
- Optional biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
- No data collection or tracking
- Complete control over your information

### 🎯 Perfect For

- Beginners starting their fasting journey
- Experienced fasters wanting better tracking
- Anyone looking to improve their health habits
- People who want social support for their goals

### 📱 What's Included

- Clean, intuitive interface designed for daily use
- Support for popular fasting schedules (16:8, 18:6, 20:4, 24h+)
- Comprehensive fasting history and streaks
- Achievement system to celebrate milestones
- Export data for health tracking integration

Start your mindful fasting journey today and discover the transformative power of intermittent fasting with the support of friends and science-backed insights.
