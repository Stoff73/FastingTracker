# Privacy Policy - Fasting Tracker

**Last Updated:** March 9, 2026

## Introduction

Fasting Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Display Name**: Used to personalize your experience within the app
- **Avatar Selection**: For profile customization
- **Fasting Goals**: Your personal fasting targets and preferences

### Fasting Data
- **Fasting Sessions**: Start times, end times, duration, and mood entries
- **Progress Statistics**: Streaks, completed fasts, and achievement data
- **Mood Tracking**: Emoji-based mood entries during fasting periods

### Technical Information
- **Device Information**: iOS version, device model (for app optimization)
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous app performance and crash data

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Core Functionality**: Enable fasting tracking, progress monitoring, and goal setting
- **Personalization**: Customize your experience based on your preferences
- **Social Features**: Allow you to connect and share progress with friends (when enabled)
- **Notifications**: Send relevant alerts and reminders based on your settings

### Secondary Uses
- **App Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
- **Support**: Provide customer service and technical assistance

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **All personal data is stored locally on your device**
- **No cloud storage or remote servers for personal information**
- **Data remains under your complete control**

### Security Measures
- **Biometric Authentication**: Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection
- **Encryption**: All local data is encrypted using iOS security standards
- **No Third-Party Access**: Your data is never shared with external services

## Sharing and Disclosure

### Friend Sharing
- **Opt-in Only**: Sharing with friends requires your explicit consent
- **Limited Data**: Only basic progress information is shared (when enabled)
- **User Control**: You can disable sharing at any time

### No Third-Party Sharing
- **We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties**
- **No advertising networks or analytics services access your personal information**
- **No data brokers or marketing companies receive your information**

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

## Your Privacy Rights

### Data Control
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app through the profile section
- **Modification**: Edit or update your information at any time
- **Deletion**: Remove your data by deleting the app
- **Export**: No export functionality needed as data stays on your device

### Communication Preferences
- **Notifications**: Control all notification types in app settings
- **Friend Requests**: Manage social features independently
- **Marketing**: We do not send marketing communications

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Health Information

### Not Medical Advice
- **The app provides general information about intermittent fasting**
- **Content is for educational purposes only**
- **Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice**

### Health Data Integration
- **Optional**: Integration with iOS Health app requires separate permission
- **User Controlled**: You decide what health data to share
- **Local Processing**: Health data integration happens on-device only

## Changes to This Policy
